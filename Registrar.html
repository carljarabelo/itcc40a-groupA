University Registrar

The Office of the University Registrar is responsible for keeping student academic records, documents and other related materials up to date. The Office assists in the enforcement of Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and school regulations regarding the admission, enrolment, student load, subject sequence, crediting and equivalence, promotion, graduation, transfer, suspension and dismissal of a student in coordination with the proper college/school. The Office prepares the academic calendar and consolidates the class and examination schedules; prepares and submits pertinent reports to the CHED,the Professional Regulations Commission (PRC) and the Civil Service Commission (CSC).


Among the documents/certifications which students may request from the Registrar’s Office are the following: (For corresponding fees, see Guidelines for Online Request.)


  • Transcript of Records
  • Transfer Credentials
  • Diploma/Authenticated Copy of Diploma
  • Course Description

Please note that:

  • No academic records will be released to a representative without a written authority from the student he/she represents and IDs of both student and the claimant.
  • The request will not be processed if the student has incomplete academic credentials (eg Form 138, Form 137-A, Transfer Credential, Official Transcript of Records, etc)
  • Diploma/Authenticated Copy of Diploma
  • XU graduates and former students upon their first request of documents shall need clearance from the following offices: Office of Student Affairs, Book Center, University Libraries, Guidance and Counseling, and Finance Office. Currently enrolled students will need clearance only from the Finance Office.
  • Transfer Credential is released only once.
  • An Affidavit of Loss is required for re-issuance of diploma.

GENERAL PROCEDURE FOR ENROLLMENT
First Semester AY 2022-2023 (2201)

FRESHMEN, TRANSFEREES, PROFESSIONAL EDUCATION, & SECOND COURSERS
Undergraduate Degrees

SCHEDULE:

Regular Enrollment:                July 11-30, 2022
Delayed Enrollment:               August 1-13, 2022
Last Day of Admissions:         August 6, 2022

PROCEDURE:

1. Pay down payment.

Pay a minimum of Php 3,000.00 down payment at the Finance Office or any of the accredited payment centers found in this link. (https://www.xu.edu.ph/payment-centers-finance)

2. Accomplish enrollment form.

Accomplish the enrollment form. Make sure to accurately supply the necessary details asked in the enrollment form.

  • Incoming Freshmen and Transferees: https://forms.gle/3YHHji731UJ91oBKA)
  • Professional Education and Second Coursers (XU Graduates Only): https://forms.gle/q5eMmqS8LA9pcVJs6

3. For incoming freshmen and transferees, submit hardcopy of SHS Report Card (for Freshmen) or Transfer Credential (for Transferees).

Submit your report card (with remarks eligible for admission to college and duly signed by the School Principal) or Transfer Credential to the Office of the University Registrar, Lucas Hall Ground Floor.

4. Wait for status update and further instructions via email.

After completing the form for enrollment, wait for the confirmation email to be sent to the email address you used in the enrollment form.

UPPERCLASSMEN
First Semester, AY 2022-2023
Undergraduate Degrees

SCHEDULE:
Regular Enrollment:    July 18-30, 2022 (Self-service enrollment)
Delayed Enrollment:   August 1-10, 2022 (Self-service enrollment)
                                    August 11-13, 2022 (Administrative enrollment)

PROCEDURE:

1. Pay down payment.

Pay a minimum of Php 3,000.00 down payment at the Finance Office or any of the accredited payment centers found in this link. (https://www.xu.edu.ph/payment-centers-finance)

2. Accomplish enrollment form.

Accomplish the enrollment form of your respective college. Please use your My XU Email in accessing the enrollment forms to avoid problems with the email address you will provide. Form links are found below.

3. Wait for term activation.

After completing the form for enrollment, you will be cleared by the Finance Office and Dean. Once cleared by both offices, you will then be term activated. Wait for the notice for your term activation.

4. Enroll courses in your prescribed study load.

Once term activated, proceed to self-service enrollment and enroll ALL the courses in your approved study load.

5. Verify enrolled courses.

Make sure you are enrolled in ALL your courses for the semester.
